abstract: >-
  NeuChain is a high-performance permissioned blockchain system that eliminates the explicit ordering phase by making transaction ordering implicit through deterministic execution. With optimizations such as asynchronous block generation and pipelining, it substantially improves throughput and latency over Hyperledger Fabric and other state-of-the-art high-performance blockchains.
